03 : About me

Hi there! I'm Kalindu Liyanage Don, a 29-year-old living in Canada's breathtaking scenery. My career path first took me into the fast-paced world of sales and marketing, where I developed my interpersonal, communication, and strategic thinking abilities.

My relentless interest about technology led me to make the crucial decision to change gears and explore the world of Python programming. For someone who has always had a strong urge to learn a programming language and investigate the almost endless possibilities it offered, it was a logical next step.

I started my journey in Python after having a life-changing experience with the Zero to Mastery platform's Complete Python Developer 2024 course. This extensive course stoked my enthusiasm for coding and problem-solving while also giving me a strong foundation in Python. I really believe that Zero to Mastery is among the greatest resources for learning Python, and I'm appreciative of the information and abilities it has given me.

Now, as a job seeker with a newfound expertise in Python, this portfolio website serves as a testament to my commitment to continuous learning and growth. Under the 'Works' tab, you'll find a collection of projects that showcase my dedication, creativity, and technical proficiency.

I am excited about the endless possibilities that Python programming brings, and I am eager to contribute my skills to innovative projects and collaborative teams. Join me on this journey of exploration and transformation.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio. Feel free to explore my projects, discover my skills, and connect with me. Let's create something amazing together!
